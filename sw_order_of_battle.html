1. Imperial Infantry Units
The Galactic Empire fields two parallel ground forces at 2 BBY — the Imperial Army (regular infantry) and the Stormtrooper Corps (elite shock troops). Both appear in battles simultaneously. Regular Army troopers are more common in garrison duties. Stormtroopers are deployed for high-priority operations and front-line assault.

Stormtrooper Legion
The Stormtrooper Corps uses the term Legion instead of Battlegroup. A Legion = 9,216 stormtroopers commanded by a Legion Commander. The 501st Legion is the most famous — Vader's Fist. Deployed as independent units attached to Army formations.
5. Rebel Alliance Infantry Units
The Rebel Alliance fields a diverse mix of unit types — regular rebel troopers form the bulk, SpecForce specialists handle high-priority operations, and irregular resistance fighters swell numbers in contested sectors. At 2 BBY the Alliance is still building — Phoenix Squadron's forces are predominantly SpecForce with minimal heavy armor.

Rebel Organization Note
The Rebel Alliance does not maintain rigid military hierarchy like the Empire. Units are often formed ad-hoc for specific operations. A "Company" might be 100 fighters rather than a precise 144. The numbers above are the intended structure — actual headcounts fluctuate based on losses and recruitment. Phoenix Squadron at 2 BBY operates closer to the battalion level with SpecForce Company attachments.

ISB Designations
Imperial Security Bureau agents use Agent, Senior Agent, and Section Chief designations — not military ranks. Agent Kallus is ISB Agent, not Lieutenant. ISB agents outrank military officers in intelligence matters regardless of their technical seniority. An ISB Agent can countermand a General's orders in their operational domain.
