Sector Adjacency & Hyperlane Reference
Star Wars Grand Strategy — 2 BBY — Movement Planning
How to use this document: When declaring a Move action in Phase 1, the fleet or force can only travel to sectors listed as adjacent — connected by hyperlane in one jump. A standard Move action (1 AP) moves forces to any directly adjacent sector. Moving two sectors away requires either 2 AP across 2 turns, or a Rating 5 commander spending 1 PW to move any distance in a single turn.

Hyperlane types affect strategic chokepoints: Major hyperlanes are the fastest routes and the most heavily patrolled. Minor routes take longer but are harder to blockade. Secret routes are known only to smugglers and intelligence operatives — they cannot be blocked conventionally.
MAJOR HYPERLANE — Fast, high traffic, blockadeable
MINOR ROUTE — Slower, less patrolled
SECRET ROUTE — Smuggler lanes, cannot be conventionally blocked
