1. Imperial Capital Ships
Capital ships are the backbone of Imperial fleet power. At 2 BBY the ISD-I is the primary weapon. ISD-II and larger vessels are rare at this stage of the war. All confirmed present in AotR.

10. AotR Battle Setup Notes
General Setup
SettingRecommendation
Game ModeSkirmish — Space Battle. Set up your fleet manually before starting.
Starting PositionPlace capital ships in formation before match starts. ISDs in line, escorts on flanks, carriers at rear.
Retreat TimesAotR gives Rebels shorter retreat time — reflects their hit-and-run doctrine. Don't expect Rebels to stand and fight to the death.
Fighter FlightsShips no longer launch full squadrons — only half. TIE Fighters, Interceptors, and Bombers are the exception — they always launch full squadrons.
Capital Ship TurningLarge ships turn very slowly in AotR. ISDs cannot quickly respond to flanking attacks — use this as the Rebels.
Ion CannonsIon cannon hits have much bigger impact in AotR than vanilla. A Y-wing ion hit can significantly disable an ISD system temporarily.
Cinematic Battle Tips
ScenarioSetup for Best Cinematic Result
ISD vs CR90 chaseSet CR90s to flee toward map edge. ISD pursues. Film TIE Fighters intercepting the fleeing corvettes.
Y-wing torpedo runSend Y-wings in from the ISD flank. Film the torpedoes impacting shield generators. Have A-wings cover them from TIE Fighters.
Interdictor trapPlace Interdictors at map corners. Film Rebel ships attempting to jump and failing — pulling back into battle.
Hammerhead ramDrive a Hammerhead directly into an ISD flank at full speed. The collision and resulting explosion is cinematic gold.
TIE swarm interceptSend 12 TIE Fighters directly at the Quasar Fire carrier. Film A-wings scrambling to intercept before TIEs reach it.
Capital ship duelOne ISD vs one Dreadnaught at close range. Both slugging it out. Film from the side at medium distance for scale.
Filming Tip — Camera Distance
For capital ship battles film at medium distance — close enough to see weapon impacts but far enough to see the full ship silhouette. For fighter engagements go close — the chaos and scale only reads at close range. Switch between the two throughout the battle for variety.
